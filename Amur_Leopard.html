This is a Amur Leopard. It is the rarest big cat in the world!

Basic Information Of Amur Leopard

  1. It can run at speeds of up to 37 miles per hour and leap more than 19 feet horizontally and up to 10 feet vertically.
  2. It is adapted to cold weather and its fur changes with the seasons.
  3. It is solitary and carries and hides unfinished kills to avoid other predators.
  4. It is slightly smaller than other types of leopard and has slightly longer legs.
