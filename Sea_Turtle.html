This is a Sea Turtle! It is an ancient reptile!

Basic Information Of Sea Turtle

  1. It is estimated that only one out of 1,000 hatchlings survives to be an adult.
  2. The sex of sea turtles, like other reptiles, depends on the temperature in the nest.
  3. Sea turtles dont have a favorite food (though most will eat jellyfish).
  4. Some sea turtles migrate very long distances while others stay close to home.
