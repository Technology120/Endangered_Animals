This is a African Bush Elephant. It is the largest land animal!

Basic Information Of African Bush Elephant

  1. It is the largest living terrestrial animal, reaching up to 3.96 m in height and 10.4 t in weight.
  2. It is distributed across 37 African countries and inhabits various habitats, such as forests, grasslands, woodlands, wetlands and agricultural land.
  3. It is a herbivore that consumes up to 350 pounds of vegetation per day, including grass, leaves, bark, fruit, and foliage.
  4. It has large ears that are shaped like Africa and can grow up to 2 m. The ears help regulate body temperature and communicate.
